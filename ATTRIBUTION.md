## Attribution Rules

- Upstream copyright and license notices must be preserved.

- Substantial transformations should describe what changed for Codex compatibility.
- When an asset is copied nearly verbatim, its upstream source path must be explicit.

## Adaptation Intent

The goal of this repository is not to present upstream Claude-targeted agent files as native Codex
artifacts. The goal is to adapt their useful role definitions and workflows into Codex-oriented
skills, plugins, and guidance examples.
